About EBSB

The Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) initiative aims to enhance interaction & promote mutual understanding between people of different states/UTs through the concept of state/UT pairing. At IIT Jammu, our club brings this vision to life through various cultural, educational, and technical events.

Club Highlights

Cultural Events

Experience the vibrancy of India through festivals like Onam, Pongal, Janmashtami, and Baisakhi celebrated right on campus.

Peer Learning Circles

Interactive sessions where students share knowledge about regional histories, languages, and unique traditions.

Technical Events

Bridging culture with technology through Hackathons, Workshops, and challenges like the Arduino EVM Challenge.
