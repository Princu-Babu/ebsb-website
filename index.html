About EBSB

The Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) initiative aims to enhance interaction & promote mutual understanding between people of different states/UTs through the concept of state/UT pairing. At IIT Jammu, our club brings this vision to life through various cultural, educational, and technical events.
